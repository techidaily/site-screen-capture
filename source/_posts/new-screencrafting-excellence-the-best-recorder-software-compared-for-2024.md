## Screen Record on Windows with Built-in Program - Xbox Game Bar

There is no need to install any app on Windows desktop or laptop if you want to record your gameplay or any videos. Microsoft owns an in-built screen recorder - [Xbox Game Bar](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9nzkpstsnw4p?hl=zh-cn&gl=US).

It can screen recording with audio and your voice at the same time. You can also capture screenshots as well as instant replay. Furthermore, it supports monitoring your system performance while recording through its widgets.

**Step 1:** Press '**Windows + G**' keys together to launch Xbox Game Bar on your Windows 11 PC. The shortcut is the same on Windows 10.

![](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/use-xbox-game-bar-on-windows-11-step1.jpg)

**Step 2:** Once you are ready with the screen you want to record, click on the '**Record**' button on the Capture toolbar. Alternatively, you can use '**Windows + Alt + R**' keys together to start recording.

![](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/use-xbox-game-bar-on-windows-11-step2.jpg)

**Step 3:** Press '**Windows + Alt + R**' keys when you want to stop recording.

![](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/use-xbox-game-bar-on-windows-11-step3.jpg)

**Step 4:** The recorded video will be available under '**Widgets > Gallery**' or '**Captures > Videos**' section.

![](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/use-xbox-game-bar-on-windows-11-step4.jpg)
